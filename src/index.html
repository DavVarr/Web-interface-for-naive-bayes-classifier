Naive bayes classifier
Dark mode
This site was made to provide an interface to train and use a Naive-Bayes classifier on various documents or posts. In this instance, some finance related tweets are provided. You can choose either to teach or classify a tweet by clicking on it after selecting one of the 3 provided modes. the progress bar will fill as the provided tweets are classified. You can switch between pages by clicking on the numbers in the pagination bar below.
